# eIDAS Tutorial

Introductory and progressively growing project about the European eIDAS framework, designed as a basis for documentation, educational materials, and future expansions on digital identity, electronic trust, and qualified trust services.

## Language Navigation

- Spanish edition: [LegalIntermediaSL/eIDAS](https://github.com/LegalIntermediaSL/eIDAS)
- English edition: [LegalIntermediaSL/eIDAS-en](https://github.com/LegalIntermediaSL/eIDAS-en)

Note: language editions may not always be perfectly synchronized in structure, wording, or update date.

## Objective

This repository aims to build a clear tutorial about:

- what eIDAS is and why it matters in Europe
- how electronic identification works in the European framework
- what trust services are
- the difference between electronic signatures, electronic seals, timestamps, and registered electronic delivery
- the changes introduced by eIDAS 2.0 and the European Digital Identity Wallet

The idea is to keep the content understandable for legal, technical, and business profiles.
