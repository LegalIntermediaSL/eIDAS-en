# 27. Risk-Based Decision Matrix

## Introduction

The right trust solution depends heavily on risk.

| Risk Level | Typical Approach |
| --- | --- |
| Low | Simpler mechanisms may be acceptable |
| Medium | Stronger authentication and clearer evidence may be needed |
| High | Qualified services, timestamps, validation, and preservation become more important |

## Summary

A risk-based approach is often more useful than choosing tools by label alone.
