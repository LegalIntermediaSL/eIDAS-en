# 14. Advanced eIDAS 2.0 Glossary

## Introduction

This glossary gathers concepts that become especially relevant in the newer eIDAS 2.0 context.

## European Digital Identity Wallet

The wallet-based identity model promoted in the newer European framework.

## Relying Party

An entity that receives, verifies, or relies on digital identity data, attributes, credentials, or trust-related evidence.

## Attribute

A piece of information about a person or entity that may be asserted, verified, or reused in trusted digital contexts.

## Credential

A structured digital statement that can be presented and verified within an identity or trust ecosystem.

## Interoperability

The ability of systems and actors to understand and use trust-related mechanisms in a compatible way.

## Summary

The advanced glossary helps readers move from the original eIDAS categories into the broader identity vocabulary of the newer framework.
