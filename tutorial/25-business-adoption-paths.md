# 25. Business Adoption Paths

## Introduction

Organizations rarely adopt eIDAS-related mechanisms all at once. Adoption usually happens progressively.

## Typical Path

1. identify the relevant process
2. assess risk and evidential needs
3. choose the appropriate trust level
4. implement provider and workflow decisions
5. preserve and review evidence

## Summary

Business adoption works best when it follows process priorities, risk, and evidential needs rather than technology hype alone.
