# 12. Institutional Actors and Supervisors

## Introduction

The eIDAS ecosystem does not work through technology and legal text alone. It also depends on institutions, supervisory bodies, providers, and relying entities.

## Main European Actors

At EU level, key actors include:

- the European Parliament and the Council
- the European Commission
- implementation and coordination structures linked to the European framework

## Member States

Member States play a central role because they:

- supervise providers under their jurisdiction
- maintain national trusted lists
- implement the operational side of the framework

## Supervisory Authorities

Each national framework must include competent supervisory structures for trust services.

## Market and Operational Actors

The wider ecosystem also includes:

- trust service providers
- conformity and evaluation bodies
- users
- relying parties
- wallet-related actors in the newer framework

## Summary

The eIDAS ecosystem is institutional as well as technical. Understanding the actors helps explain how supervision, interoperability, and trust are made operational.
