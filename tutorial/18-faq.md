# 18. Frequently Asked Questions About eIDAS

## Is every digital signature a qualified signature

No. The qualified signature is a specific reinforced category under eIDAS.

## Is a non-qualified service automatically invalid

No. A non-qualified service may still be useful and legally relevant depending on context and risk.

## Is a wallet the same as a certificate

No. A wallet and a certificate are connected concepts in some scenarios, but they are not the same thing.

## Is eIDAS only about signatures

No. It also covers electronic identification and a broader range of trust services.

## Why do trusted lists matter

Because they help verify the official status of qualified providers and services.
