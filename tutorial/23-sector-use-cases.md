# 23. Sector-Based Use Cases

## Introduction

Different sectors use eIDAS-related tools in different ways.

## Financial Sector

Identity, customer onboarding, and evidential robustness are often central.

## Education

Credentials, identity proofing, and verifiable records are especially relevant.

## Public Sector

Procedural trust, documentary issuance, and citizen-facing access are frequent priorities.

## Healthcare and Regulated Contexts

Higher levels of assurance and stronger evidential needs may be more common.

## Summary

Sector-based cases help show how the same framework can support different operational priorities.
