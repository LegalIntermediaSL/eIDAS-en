# 22. Preservation and Validation

## Introduction

Creating evidence is only part of the challenge. Trust also depends on being able to validate and preserve that evidence over time.

## Validation

Validation helps determine whether a signature, seal, certificate, or related element can still be trusted and correctly interpreted.

## Preservation

Preservation matters because digital evidence may need to remain meaningful years after the original action took place.

## Why This Matters

Without preservation and validation, even a strong initial trust mechanism may lose value later.

## Summary

Validation and preservation are essential to the long-term evidential usefulness of digital trust mechanisms.
