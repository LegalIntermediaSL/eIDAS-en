# 20. Brief eIDAS Timeline

## Introduction

The eIDAS framework is best understood as part of a broader European evolution in digital trust.

## Timeline

1. earlier European digital-signature frameworks prepared the ground
2. Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 established the eIDAS framework
3. implementation and supervisory practice developed across Member States
4. the newer reform phase led to what is commonly called eIDAS 2.0
5. the European Digital Identity Wallet became a central feature of the new phase

## Summary

eIDAS should be seen as an evolving framework rather than a static legal text.
