# 15. Business and Public Administration Compared

## Introduction

The same eIDAS concepts may be used very differently in business environments and in public administration.

## Business Perspective

Businesses often focus on:

- efficiency
- contractual certainty
- customer onboarding
- evidential robustness where disputes are possible

## Public-Sector Perspective

Public administrations often focus on:

- legal formality
- procedural guarantees
- citizen access
- institutional trust and official evidence

## Why the Comparison Matters

The comparison helps explain why the same tool may be excessive in one case and insufficient in another.

## Summary

Business and administration share the eIDAS framework but often apply it with different priorities, constraints, and evidential expectations.
