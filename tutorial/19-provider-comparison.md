# 19. Comparison Between Qualified and Non-Qualified Providers

## Introduction

The distinction between qualified and non-qualified providers is one of the most practical distinctions in the eIDAS ecosystem.

| Aspect | Qualified Provider | Non-Qualified Provider |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Legal status | Reinforced legal category | General trust-service provision |
| Supervision | Stronger supervisory framework | Not the same qualified framework |
| Trusted-list relevance | Central | Not the same official qualified status |
| Typical use | Higher-assurance scenarios | Broader general use depending on case |

## Summary

The comparison helps explain why provider status matters when legal assurance and evidence become relevant.
