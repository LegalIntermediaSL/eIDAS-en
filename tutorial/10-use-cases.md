# 10. Real Use Cases

## Introduction

eIDAS becomes easier to understand when it is connected to actual organizational and documentary scenarios.

## Typical Business Use Cases

In business environments, eIDAS-related mechanisms are often relevant for:

- signing contracts
- onboarding customers
- issuing corporate documents
- preserving digital evidence

## Typical Public-Sector Use Cases

In public administration, the framework is often relevant for:

- digital procedures
- citizen identification
- documentary issuance
- evidential communications

## Why Use Cases Matter

Use cases show that the main question is rarely theoretical. It is usually about choosing the right level of trust and evidence for a specific process.

## Summary

Real use cases make it easier to connect the legal categories of eIDAS with practical decisions in business and administration.
