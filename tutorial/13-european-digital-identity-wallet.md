# 13. The European Digital Identity Wallet

## Introduction

The European Digital Identity Wallet is one of the most visible developments in the newer phase of the eIDAS framework.

## What the Wallet Is

In simple terms, it is a wallet-based identity environment intended to support:

- electronic identification
- management of credentials and attributes
- selective data sharing
- use of trusted digital identity in public and private services

## Why It Matters

The wallet matters because it moves the European identity model toward a more practical and user-facing approach.

## What It Is Not

It should not be reduced to a simple folder of digital files. The concept is broader and involves identity, attributes, issuers, holders, and relying parties.

## Summary

The European Digital Identity Wallet is a central piece of the new digital identity phase in Europe and a key part of the post-reform landscape.
