# 50. Spain: National Context

## Introduction

Although eIDAS is directly applicable as an EU regulation, practical work in Spain still requires understanding the national supervisory and documentary context.

## Key Point

eIDAS is not transposed in the strict sense. Instead, it is directly applicable and complemented by national rules and structures where relevant.

## Main Spanish Reference

For this project, a central Spanish reference is Law 6/2020 on certain aspects of electronic trust services.

## Summary

The Spanish context matters especially for supervision, official sources, and practical consultation.
