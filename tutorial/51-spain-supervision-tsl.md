# 51. Spain: Supervision, TSL, and Providers

## Introduction

One of the most practical questions in the Spanish context is where to verify whether a provider or service holds qualified status.

## Core Elements

- national supervision
- official provider references
- the Spanish trusted list (TSL)

## Why the TSL Matters

The Spanish trusted list is a central official source when a formal verification of qualified status is needed.

## Useful Official Links

- [Law 6/2020 in the BOE](https://www.boe.es/buscar/act.php?id=BOE-A-2020-14046)
- [Qualified providers](https://sedeaplicaciones.minetur.gob.es/prestadores/)
- [Non-qualified providers](https://sedeaplicaciones.minetur.gob.es/Prestadores/NoCualificados.aspx)
- [Spanish TSL](https://sedediatid.mineco.gob.es/Prestadores/TSL/TSL.xml)

## Summary

In Spain, official verification of provider or service status should rely on supervision-related sources and the trusted-list system.
