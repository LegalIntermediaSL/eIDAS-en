# 43. Terminology Criteria for the Project

## Introduction

This document sets some terminology rules to keep the tutorial coherent.

## Core Criteria

- `eIDAS` may refer strictly to Regulation 910/2014 or more broadly to the framework when context allows
- `eIDAS 2.0` is used as a practical expression for the reform phase linked to Regulation (EU) 2024/1183
- `simple electronic signature` is accepted as a didactic expression, while noting that the formal broad category is electronic signature
- `trusted list (TSL)` is the preferred full first mention
- `European wallet` may be used as a short form of the European Digital Identity Wallet

## Summary

Stable terminology improves clarity, consistency, and extensibility.
